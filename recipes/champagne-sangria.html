Champagne Sangria

Recipe courtesy of Unknown

Ingredients

  • 1 Granny Smith apple diced
  • 1 Red Delicious apple diced
  • 1 Bartlett pear diced
  • 1 Sliced orange
  • 2 Sliced limes
  • 1-cup simple syrup (equal amounts sugar and water heated until sugar is dissolved)
  • 3/4 pints orange juice
  • 3/4 cup brandy
  • 3/4 cup triple sec

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients and gently mix. Place in a container and refrigerate. Serve base with equal parts Sparkling wine or Champagne
