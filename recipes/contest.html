Brisket Bob’s Greatest BBQ Contest

How good is your barbeque? Pretty good? Then send in a recipe to enter and win our contest of best pitmaster in New Orleans. Submit your sizzling hot or ultra-cool recipes for a chance to win 3 great prizes.

Recipe Contest Rules and Guidelines

Three winners will be chosen. The grand prize is a $25 gift certificate to amazon.com. The 2 runners up each win a $10 gift certificate to Amazon.com.

  1. Each recipe must be submitted between July 1st 2010 and midnight July 31st 2010.
  2. Each recipe must contain at least one of Whole Food’s signature ingredients.
  3. All the ingredients must be available at Whole Food’s.
  4. Recipes must be your own creation and all submissions to the site become property of Whole Food’s Fan.com
  5. Submitters must be 21 years or older and reside the United States. Entries will be judged on creativity, taste, and general appeal.
