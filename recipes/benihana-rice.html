Benihana Fried Rice

Recipe courtesy of Unknown

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice, uncooked
  • 5 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup onion, chopped
  • 1 cup carrot, chopped
  • ⅔ cup green onion, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons sesame seeds
  • 5 eggs
  • 5 tablespoons soy sauce
  • salt
  • pepper

Instructions

  1. Scramble eggs and chop after cooking. chop onion, green onion and carrots until done and mix with chopped scrambled egg. Break off chunks of steamed rice and mix with vegetable and egg mixture. Add sesame seeds and salt and pepper. Stir in cream butter and soy sauce into the mixture.
