Bread Dip Mix

Recipe courtesy of Unknown

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon crushed red pepper
  • 1-tablespoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon dried rosemary
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • 1 tablespoon dried parsley
  • 1-tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1-teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Grind together
  2. To use, put 1 tablespoon of mixture in a small bowl. Pour extra virgin olive oil over and mix together. Dip warm sourdough French bread into the mixture.
