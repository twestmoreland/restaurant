Bourbon Steak

Recipe courtesy of Unknown

Serves 2

Ingredients

  • 2 tenderloin steaks (1 1/2 inches thick)
  • 2 tablespoons bourbon
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1/4 tablespoon freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Combine bourbon, brown sugar and pepper; rub over steaks. Preheat grill.
  2. Place steaks on grill and cook for 13 minutes (rare) to 17 minutes (medium), turning once. Season to taste with salt and serve.
